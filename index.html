By using LearnX10, you commit to the following principles:

1. No Automation or Bots
Automated scripts, bots, or tools to farm XP are strictly prohibited and will result in permanent ban.
2. One Account Per Person
Creating multiple accounts to gain advantages is against our policy and both accounts will be banned.
3. No Sharing of Unlocked Resources

Harassment, hate speech, or abusive behavior toward other members or admins is not tolerated.
5. No Tampering with Security
Attempting to bypass security measures, inspect elements, or reverse-engineer the platform triggers an automatic permanent ban.
6. Giveaway Integrity
All giveaway entries must be legitimate. Any suspicious activity in giveaways results in disqualification.

Violations of these policies result in XP deduction, temporary suspension, or permanent ban at admin discretion.
